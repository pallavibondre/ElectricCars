The electric vehicle power source is the battery which acts as a "gas tank" and supplies the electric motor with the energy necessary to move the vehicle. An electric car is an automobile that is propelled by one or more electric motors, using energy stored in rechargeable batteries.

Electric cars run on electrical energy. Electric cars have an electric motor that is powered by a fuel cell. Some batteries from the electric cars can last for at least 12 to 15 years. There are four kinds of batteries: lithium-ion, nickel- metal hydride, lead-acid, and ultracapacitors. Lithium-ion batteries are mostly used for portable electronics and electric vechiles. One advantage for the Nickel-Metal Hydribe battery is that Energy's density can be changed into long run times or reduction in the space necessary for the battery. Lead acid was the first recharable battery invented. Lead acid is commenly made out of antimony,calicum,tin,and selenium.
